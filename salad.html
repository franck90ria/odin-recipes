Caesar Salad

Description

The Caesar Salad is a dish that was invented by an italian man. Traditionally composed only by a cold and crunchy romaine lettuce, a craggy croutons, some Parmesan flakes and a creamy and briny dressing; nowadays exist many variations of this course.

One of this alternatives include a tasty topping: grilled chicken!

Ingredients

DRESSING

  • 6 anchovy fillet packet in oil, drained
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • Kosher salt
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
    (plus more)
  • ¾ teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoon finely grated Parmesan
  • Ground black pepper

CROUTONS

  • 3 cups torn 1" pieces country bread
    (obviously with crust)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

CHICKEN CRACKLING

  • 2 chicken breasts with skin on
    (the key)
  • drizzle olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Sea salt
  • Fresh black pepper

ASSEMBLY

  • 3 romaine hearts
    (leaves separated)
  • Parmesan flakes
    (touch of class)

Steps

Dressing preparation

  1. Chop together anchovy fillets, garlic and pinch of salt
  2. Use the side of a knife blade to mash this minced into a paste
  3. Scrape into a medium bowl
  4. Whisk in egg yolks, 2 tablespoons lemon juice and Dijon mustard
  5. Add slowly, first olive oil, then vegetable oil
  6. Make a thick and glossy dressing
  7. Whisk in Parmesan
  8. Season with salt, pepper and more lemon juice
    (if desired)

HINT:
Can be made Ⅰ day ahead

Chicken

BREASTS
  1. Heat oven to 200°C/180°C fan/gas
  2. Remove the skin from the chicken and set aside
  3. Cover the chicken with cling film
  4. Bash out to flatten slightly
  5. Put it in a large bowl with lemon juice, garlic clove, drizzle of oil, salt and pepper
  6. Set aside
SKIN
  1. Lined a baking sheet with a baking parchment
  2. Sprinkle it with the sea salt
  3. Lay chicken skin on the baking sheet
  4. Put another piece of baking parchment on top
  5. Weight it down with a baking tray
  6. Roast for 25-30 mins until crisp and golden
Comeback to BREASTS
  1. Heat a griddle pan
  2. Griddle chicken for 4-5 mins on each side
  3. Slice it into pieces and set aside

Croutons

  1. Preheat oven to 375°C
  2. Toss bread with olive oil on a baking sheet
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Bake, Tossing occasionally, until golden, 10-15 mins

Assembly

  1. Place whole romaine leaves in a large bowl
  2. Use a vegetable peeler to thinly shave an amount of Parmesan
  3. Add croutons, chicken and the crackling skin
  4. With your hands, toss gently all together and add the dressing

HINT:
For a better touch of crunchiness, you can store the romaine leaves in in the refrigerator until you're ready to toss everything together
