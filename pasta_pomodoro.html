Spaghetti Pomodoro

Description

Symbolic dish of the italian cooking, an harmonic combination of the characteristics ingredients of the Bel Paese. Spaghetti pomodoro is an institution of the italian culture, a simple recipe, but far from banal. Revisited in many versions, the tradition want that this humble dish would compose by few ingredients: fresh tomato sauce, basil and a sprinkling of Parmesan... for a plunge into pleasure!

Ingredients

SUGO POMODORO(Tomato sauce)

  • 800 g peeled plum tomato
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh Basil
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper
  • Grated Parmesan

Your favorite pasta...

  • 320 g spaghetti

PREPARATION

The protagonist: Pomodoro Sauce

  1. Sauté the peeled garlic clove in the olive oil in a large pan

    2. If you want an intense aroma, you could divide the clove in half.

    Brown the garlic for 2 minutes.

  2. Add the peeled plum tomatoes

    3. Season with salt and pepper, and reduce the heat to low. Simmer the sauce covered for about 1 hour and stir it occasionally.

    The secret to a good Salsa Pomodoro is cooking, it will have poach gently!

  3. Remove the garlic clove

  4. Purée the tomatoes with a food mill

    5. You should obtain a smooth emulsion.

  5. Put back the Salsa in the pan and add the fresh basil

    6. Cook it over low heat for a few minutes, than turn off the stove and left the pan covered.

Et voilà your Salsa Pomodoro is ready!

Cooking Pasta

  1. Put a pot fill of water on stove
  2. Wait for the water boil
  3. Season with salt
  4. Put the spaghetti in the pot
  5. Drain the pasta when spaghetti are al dente
  6. Put it in the pan with Salsa and stir it over high heat for a few moments

Plating the pasta with some tiny basil leaves, a sprinkling of Parmesan and...

bon appètit!
